MPEG Audio

The Galaxy Music System offers realtime decoding of MPEG Audio streams, currently it fully supports MPEG 1, 2 and 2.5 layers 1, 2 and 3 all bitrates and all samplingfrequencies. Support for MPEG 2 AAC Audio streams is in the works and is expected in the future. The MPEG decoding engine is custom build and highly optimized, it will use FPU, MMX or 3DNow! depending on the installed CPU. All the decoding is handled internally by Galaxy both streaming and on-the-fly decompression is possible. Please notice that licensing the Galaxy Music System does NOT incorporate an MPEG Audio license, please contact either THOMSON multimedia for high volume desktop-software or OPTICOM GmbH for low volume desktop-software. They can tell you everything you need to know about obtaining an MPEG Audio license.
